Daily Health Tips

  • Drink at least 2 liters of water daily to stay hydrated, especially in Uganda's tropical climate.

  • Use mosquito nets every night to prevent malaria, even if you don't see mosquitoes.

  • Practice 20-20-20 rule for eye health: every 20 minutes, look 20 feet away for 20 seconds.

  • Include at least one serving of leafy greens in your daily meals for essential nutrients.

  • Get at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise daily - even walking counts!
